Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE)

Determines the total number of calories you burn per day, which is crucial for managing weight, whether the goal is to lose, maintain, or gain weight.

Maximum Heart Rate (MHR)

Used to determine the target heart rate zones for cardiovascular training, which helps optimize workout intensity for different fitness goals (fat burning, cardiovascular fitness, etc.).

Protein Requirement

Ensures adequate protein intake, which is crucial for muscle repair, growth, and overall fitness.

Body Mass Index (BMI)

Assesses whether a person has a healthy body weight for a given height.
